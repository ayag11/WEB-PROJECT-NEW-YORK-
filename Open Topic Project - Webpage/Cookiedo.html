Welcome to the CookieDō Homepage! Here you will find a couple of facts about us, our location and our different flavors of edible cookie dough! Please take your time and look at our website.

CookieDō is the first home to the edible cookie dough dessert shop. Here in New York City, we are open seven days a week, 24/7. We are located: 550 LaGuardia Pl, New York, NY 10012. Our most popular flavors include birthday cake, double chocolate chip and peanut butter oreo cookie.
